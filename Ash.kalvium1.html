lemon juice

The perfect treat for every occasion

Ingredients

  • fresh lemon
  • 1 cup (240 mL) cold water
  • 2 tablespoons (24 grams) sugar, or to taste
  • A pinch of salt (optional, for flavor)
  • Ice cubes

Tools

  • squeezer or a fork
  • Glass
  • Strainer (optional)
  • Knife

Step-by-step instructions

  1. Prepare the lemon: Roll the lemon on a hard surface, pressing down with your palm. This helps soften the fruit and release more juice.
  2. Cut and squeeze: Cut the lemon in half. Squeeze the juice from each half into a glass. You can use a citrus squeezer for convenience, or squeeze by hand. If you are squeezing by hand, hold a strainer over the glass to catch any seeds.
  3. Mix the ingredients: Add the water, sugar, and an optional pinch of salt to the glass of lemon juice.
  4. Stir until dissolved: Stir the mixture thoroughly until the sugar and salt have completely dissolved.
  5. Serve chilled: Add a few ice cubes to the glass and stir. Your lemon juice is ready to be served.

NOTE

Add fresh mint: Add a few fresh mint leaves to the glass for an extra burst of freshness.
